Python Web Scrapping and NLP Modelling

During this project, I used BeautifulSoup Package to scrap the data science job details from the job seeking website and generated dataframe.

Then I used the NLP (TF/IDF Model) to convert the dataframe to numerical format

Finally I used various classification algorithms to predict the job segments and job salaries.


Technologies

  • Pandas
  • Numpy
  • Matplotlib
  • Seaborn
  • Sciki-Learn
  • BeautifulSoup
  • TF/IDF Model
  • ELI5
